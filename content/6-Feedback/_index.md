> This section captures my personal feedback after completing the internship roadmap, from AWS onboarding to chatbot integration and final regression testing.

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**
The environment was supportive and practical for learning. Team members were willing to discuss blockers, and collaboration was effective across backend, cloud, and testing tasks. I appreciated that I could work on real implementation scopes instead of only theory.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**
Mentor support was strong and practical. Instead of only giving direct answers, the mentor often guided us to reason through architecture and debugging choices. This approach helped me improve problem-solving and technical decision-making.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**
The tasks matched my AI/cloud learning direction well. Besides core backend and database work, I had opportunities to apply AI-related knowledge in chatbot, Bedrock integration, function calling, and RAG.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**
The internship offered broad technical growth:

* Database design and normalization.
* Prisma ORM integration and migration workflow.
* EC2/RDS setup and cloud connectivity.
* API design, integration debugging, and regression testing.
* Practical AI application with retrieval-enhanced chatbot responses.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**
Team culture was respectful and execution-focused. Even under tight deadlines, communication remained constructive and solution-oriented. I felt trusted as an intern to own deliverables and contribute meaningfully.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**
The internship structure was clear and gave enough flexibility to balance learning and delivery. Weekly goals and milestone-oriented execution made progress visible and measurable.

---

### Additional Questions

**Most satisfying part**

* Seeing the project evolve from planning to deployment and final stabilization.
* Completing a full cycle from design to production-like testing.
* Building an AI-enabled chatbot flow connected to real project data.

**Suggested improvements for future interns**

* Provide a standard technical template for architecture notes and release notes.
* Add a starter checklist for cloud security and cost control in early weeks.
* Allocate a dedicated session on testing strategy and automation basics.

**Would I recommend this internship?**

Yes. I would recommend it to students who want practical cloud engineering exposure with real implementation responsibilities and mentor support.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

* Continue strengthening cross-team technical reviews at milestone checkpoints.
* Add more demo days where interns present implementation decisions and lessons learned.
* I would like to continue participating in advanced phases if available.
